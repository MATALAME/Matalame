Packaging Design

This is a curated selection of client and personal projects that sit alongside my UI/UX work, showcasing how design thinking can extend across different mediums. While my portfolio focuses on creating intuitive, user-centred digital experiences, these projects allowed me to explore visual communication, branding, and packaging design. Each piece reflects a deliberate approach to colour, composition, and storytelling, highlighting how thoughtful design can create memorable, meaningful experiences—both on-screen and in the physical world.

JDJ Enterprise

Client / Personal project: Client Project

Design Type: Packaging Design

My role: Product Designer

Tools used: Adobe Photoshop

Description

This project involved designing custom box packaging for JDJ Enterprise, a startup courier company seeking a more distinctive and memorable alternative to standard brown packaging. The goal was to introduce personality and brand identity into a functional shipping box.

The walking man silhouette was used as a core visual element to symbolise movement, progress, and the company’s ongoing journey to reliably deliver parcels to customers. The final design balances practicality with character, helping the brand stand out while reinforcing its purpose.

Lucid Spirit

Client / Personal project: Personal Project

Design Type: Packaging Design

My role: Product Designer

Tools used: Adobe Photoshop

Description

Lucid Spirit is a self-initiated packaging concept created to explore luxury branding within the alcohol industry. The design was approached with a focus on exclusivity, aiming to evoke a premium, limited-edition feel through refined visual choices.

Careful attention was given to composition, tone, and overall presentation to communicate sophistication and desirability, while pushing creative boundaries beyond familiar product categories.

Fructify Cosemtics

Client / Personal project: Client Project

Design Type: Packaging Design

My role: Product Designer

Tools used: Adobe Photoshop

Description

This client project focused on packaging design for Fructify Cosmetics, a hair-care brand centred on health and wellness. The brief emphasised the need to visually communicate natural care and product benefits.

Green was selected as the primary colour to represent growth, freshness, and vitality, while leaf motifs were incorporated to reinforce themes of nature and nourishment. The final packaging design conveys a clean, healthy, and trustworthy brand identity aligned with the client’s values.

Tender Courier

Client / Personal project: Personal Project

Design Type: Packaging Design

My role: Product Designer

Tools used: Adobe Photoshop

Description

Tender Courier is a personal exploration that builds upon a previous courier packaging concept. After finalising a client version, this iteration allowed for further experimentation with colour, contrast, and brand presence.

A bold black and red palette was chosen to communicate strength, urgency, and reliability, while refining the overall visual impact. The project served as an opportunity to iterate on earlier design decisions and strengthen packaging consistency within the courier sector.
