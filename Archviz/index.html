Modern Kitchen Design

Details

Project Type Personal Project
Design Type: Interior Design
My Role: Designer & 3D Artist
Tools Used: Blender

Description

This project explores a modern, minimalist kitchen designed for a clean, open feel. The kitchen is centered around a spacious island, designed for food preparation and informal dining, while creating a sense of connectivity in the space. The layout emphasizes simplicity and functionality, with uncluttered surfaces and a cohesive color palette that balances light wood tones with white countertops. The aim was to create a space that’s practical, and stylish, with careful attention to lighting, materials, and overall composition.

Studio Apartment Digital Twin

Details

Project Type Personal Project
Design Type: Interior Design
My Role: Designer & 3D Artist
Tools Used: Blender

Description

This project presents a complete physical-to-digital visualization of a compact, multi-functional studio apartment. The aim was to accurately recreate an existing physical space as a detailed digital twin, emphasizing both spatial realism and design efficiency. The visualization highlights key areas — the living zone, compact kitchen, workspace, and bathroom — showcasing thoughtful lighting, material accuracy, and spatial composition to bring the apartment’s atmosphere to life.

Virtual Reality Documentary

Details

Project Type Personal Project
Design Type: Interior Design
My Role: Designer & 3D Artist
Tools Used: Blender

Description

This is my final-year Virtual Reality exam. We were tasked to create a VR documentary of an elderly person of our choice. For this project, I outsourced the characters and furniture for the scene. Our story takes place in the South African 90s, and I created a living room, sitting area, and kitchen to fit that mood using the outsourced models. We were also tasked to use the principles of VR such as proximity, light, and colour. I was responsible for the Title Card, Chapter 1, and Chapter 4 (Environment).
